What is the purpose of this website?

The purpose of this website is to guide new GMU students on the ethical and effective use of AI tools like ChatGPT in an academic setting. It aims to provide clear guidelines on how to integrate AI responsibly into their studies while maintaining academic integrity. By offering resources, examples, and FAQs, the site helps students understand the potential of AI as a learning aid rather than a shortcut. Ultimately, it encourages students to use AI to enhance their education while adhering to university policies.
