Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI-powered language model developed by OpenAI. It can generate human-like responses to text prompts and assist with a variety of tasks, such as answering questions, generating content, and more.

How can I use ChatGPT responsibly?

Use ChatGPT as a tool to assist your learning or work, but avoid submitting its responses as your own. Always verify the information it provides and cite it when necessary.

What is the purpose of this website?

This website is designed to educate users about ChatGPT, its ethical use, and its potential applications in productivity and learning.
