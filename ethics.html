Data Privacy

Ethical

  • Not sharing personal data in prompts
  • Using anonymized examples

Unethical

  • Entering real names, SSNs, or passwords
  • Sharing sensitive university data

Academic Honesty

Ethical

  • Using ChatGPT to brainstorm or clarify topics
  • Citing AI-generated ideas when used

Unethical

  • Copying and submitting ChatGPT output directly
  • Using it to complete take-home exams

University AI Policies

Many universities are now publishing guidelines for acceptable AI usage in academic work. Always check your course syllabus or school's honor code to see how tools like ChatGPT are permitted or restricted. When in doubt, ask your professor.
