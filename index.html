What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI-powered language model developed by OpenAI. It's designed to understand natural language prompts and generate human-like responses. Built on top of the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) architecture, ChatGPT has been trained on a vast corpus of internet text to provide helpful, informative, and contextually relevant replies across a wide range of topics.

How does it work?

ChatGPT works by predicting the next word in a sentence based on the words that came before. During its training phase, it learned to understand grammar, context, facts, and even reasoning patterns. When you ask a question, ChatGPT uses its trained neural network to generate a relevant and coherent response ‚Äî all in real time. It's like autocomplete on steroids, with deep contextual understanding!
